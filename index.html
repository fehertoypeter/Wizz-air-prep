Movements Cube ℹ️

Spatial Orientation III module.

In this module your task is to imagine the rotation of a cube. You'll be given a cube with one side marked (initial position) then you will hear several instructions on how to rotate the cube. You'll need to determine the final position of the mark based on audio recordings.

Keep the following information in mind:
- The cube can only be pivoted around two of its axis, the vertical and the lateral axis.
- The cube's position may be top, left, right, front, behind and bottom.
- The instructions you'll hear will involve moving the cube up, down, left or right.
